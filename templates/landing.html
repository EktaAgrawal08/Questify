Transforming Content into Engaging Questions



Questify is an intelligent platform that allows users to upload PDFs, Word files, or text files, and automatically generates Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) with accurate answers. Powered by the Google Gemini API, Questify ensures that the MCQs are derived solely from the uploaded content, maintaining accuracy and relevance.

Users can then download the generated MCQs as a PDF, making it an ideal tool for educators, students.
